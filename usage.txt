Welcome to Santa Claus Hunter!

Usage:
./my_hunter : launch the game.
./my_hunter -h : usage file.

The goal is to shoot a maximum of times Santa Claus without missing him !
Everytime you miss him you lose one bullet, and you got 3 lifes of 3 bullet each.

You can see your life at the top left of your screen while you play.
At the bottom right you can see your number of bullet left.
At the top left, you can see your actual score, and the biggest score ever scored.

Have fun playing!
