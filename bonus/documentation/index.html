Rules of the game.


The my_hunter santa claus hunter is a game where you need to shoot on sauta claus thanks to the aim cursor?

You got 3 lifes and 3 bullets per life.

Reached 0 bullet, if you still got one life, you'll get back your bullet and lose one life.

Reached 0 life and 0 bullet, you lose. If you scored higher than the actual record, your score will be saved inside the launcher.

Informations on your screen

If you need some help to understand how the games works.


Number of life

You can see at the top right of your screen the number of life you still have. They're represented by green heart. If you see one left on your screens, means its your last one.



Number of bullet

You can see how many bullet you have left for this life. If when you shoot, you toudh the santa claus, you get back the bullet. They're represented by green and yellow gifts. If you see one left on your screens, means its your last one.


Score and biggest score

At the top left of your screen, you can see your actual score and the biggest record actually score on the game.
